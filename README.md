This coding project explores the relationship between humans and artificial systems through an interactive webpage inspired by the theme of my illustration project.

The webpage shows a girl standing in a dreamlike environment with a keyhole on her forehead. At the top of the page, viewers are prompted to “Try to put the key into the keyhole”. A key-shaped cursor encourages interaction with the character. When the keyhole is clicked, a clicking sound effect is triggered and a second illustration fills the screen, revealing the girl's inner world.

I wanted the interaction to feel like unlocking someone's thoughts, emotions, and memories rather than simply viewing an image. The illustrations are intentionally displayed at a large scale so viewers can focus on the visual details and become immersed in the experience.

AI was used to help build parts of the website, including interaction, sound functions, and troubleshooting code during development.
